Unleash the Extraordinary

Our event capabilities go far beyond those of a typical caterer. With our depth of network and resources we can design, conceptualize, produce and manage large-scale events such as fundraiser galas, award shows, movie premieres, product unveilings, grand openings, live performances and more. If mind-blowing spectacles and immersive guest experiences are what you’re after, the specialists at 24 Carrots can pull it off on an epic scale!

A Proven Track Record

We are honored to have worked with some of the most recognizable companies, organizations, and performers in the world. Here are just a few: Red Bull, Lexus, Nordstrom, MTV, McLaren, Ferrari, Macy’s, Paul McCartney, Miley Cyrus, and John Mayer, along with so many others.
