The Table

At 24 Carrots, we’re best known for our diverse menu and delectable food that makes guests savor every bite. Our professionally-trained chefs are connoisseurs who have devoted their lives to perfecting their craft and preparing food that looks just as good as it tastes.

the 24c way

Hand-Picked, Thoughtfully Curated

At 24 Carrots, all of our food is cooked fresh and assembled by hand. Unlike other caterers that keep food in holding boxes, which degrades quality, 24 Carrots cooks on-site to ensure our food is hot, fresh, and delicious. Our dedication to quality is unrivaled, and we’re incredibly proud of the efforts we make to exceed even the highest expectations.

Our large state-of-the-art kitchen located in our Southern California headquarters provides us with the space and resources needed to prepare outstanding food for any occasion. Our passion is evident from the first bite your guests take all the way through to their last.

Menus That Are All You!

Everyone has their own unique tastes and preferences. That’s why the friendly and flexible team at 24 Carrots works closely with you to devise the perfect personalized menu for your event. As the food and beverage experts, we’ll guide you through curating a menu that reflects you, while proactively planning for any special needs and requests.

Menu Tastings

Private & Personalized

At 24 Carrots, we don’t think of you as just another customer. You have unique tastes and preferences, so when you visit us for a tasting of our acclaimed catering menu it should be just as unique!

While it’s common for other caterers to offer pre-scheduled group tastings with a generic menu set by the chef, we take a different approach. Our tastings are private and personalized just for you.

The Attention You Deserve!

Our intimate menu tastings are part of what makes 24 Carrots unlike any other caterer. You are joined by your personal Catering & Event Specialist, who guides you through the process of selecting your menu, as well as a private server, who ensures your experience is just right. Since we don’t do group sessions, we’re able to take the time to get to know you and focus all of our attention on perfecting the meal for your event.

Consistent From Tasting to Table

During your private menu tasting, you’ll be served the same meal your guests will enjoy on the day of your event. Unlike some caterers that lure you in with an appetizing tasting and then disappoint with a lesser quality meal when it really matters, 24 Carrots chefs ensure your meal will taste exactly the same.

You’ll get to see full table mockups for your event too, including china and linens, so you can immerse yourself in the experience. And since you’ll be trying a lot of different foods during your tasting, we’ll even pack up the leftovers for you to enjoy later!
