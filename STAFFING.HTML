Event staffing

Unparalleled Expertise

The hand-selected team of professionals at 24 Carrots are just as important to our reputation as our gourmet food. While our staff is certainly the best at what they do, experience is worth little without a great attitude. We take the time to get to know you, because working with good people who genuinely care about the success of your event makes for an experience you’ll feel confident and comfortable with from start to finish!

service excellence

Always Going
Above & Beyond

You’d be surprised how many tiny details go into planning a successful event. At 24 Carrots, our goal is to never make you have to find out. When you choose to work with us, you are assigned a personal Catering & Event Manager. We walk you through menu selection, diagrams, linen selection, tastings, and event details so you know exactly what to expect with no surprises along the way. In the end, our main objective is always your peace of mind, because service excellence comes standard at 24 Carrots.

World Class Service

The 24 Carrots team is a select group of highly experienced professionals who are here to serve you. But, experience is worth little without a great attitude. Our team members are courteous and gracious, always greeting you with a smile. Every event is assigned a hand-selected Event Lead who ensures all aspects of your function are covered.

24 Carrots staff always arrive on-time and are ready before your event’s scheduled start time. Servers, dressed in discreet black uniforms (other dress options are also available), are all ServSafe certified for food safety and quality. All members of our staff are prepared and motivated to perform at exceptional levels. Extensive service training is conducted throughout the year to maintain a consistent and enjoyable experience for our clients.
